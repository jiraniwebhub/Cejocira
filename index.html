About Cejocira Enterprises

Cejocira Enterprises is a licensed general supplies company based in Nairobi. We specialise in supplying high-quality office stationery, printing solutions, branded merchandise and equipment support to clients throughout Kenya.

Our team values trust, speed and clarity. We help schools, businesses and organizations get the supplies they need on time, with reliable delivery and transparent pricing.
